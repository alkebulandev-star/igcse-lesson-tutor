The personal tutor

It teaches the method first, then asks the check question.

Lesson Teacher does not lead with the answer. It works through the method, shows the working, and asks one short question at the end to test whether you followed. Every system prompt is locked to the official Cambridge or Edexcel specification by subject code — 0580, 0620, 9709 and the rest.

Common questions

Common questions.

Is the AI tutor aligned to the official Cambridge / Edexcel syllabus?
Yes. Every system prompt locks the tutor to the current Cambridge IGCSE, Edexcel IGCSE, Cambridge International A-Level or Edexcel International A-Level specification by subject code (e.g. Maths 0580, Physics 9702). AO1 / AO2 / AO3 weighting is encoded so marking matches the actual mark scheme.
Which year groups do you cover?
Reception (EYFS) through Year 13. That covers KS1 (Year 1 — 2), KS2 (Year 3 — 6, includes SATs & 11+ practice), KS3 (Year 7 — 9, Cambridge Lower Secondary Checkpoint), KS4 (Year 10 — 11, IGCSE) and KS5 (Year 12 — 13, AS & A-Level).
Will my child see content from the right country?
Yes. Lesson Teacher is British-curriculum-only — no mixed-curriculum content. Examples, currency (£), units (SI with imperial notes) and case studies are British and international. Cultural references are the Premier League, the BBC, the NHS, the National Grid, the M25, the Lake District and Hinkley Point C.
How is student data handled?
GDPR and KCSIE aligned. No third-party advertising trackers. API keys live server-side only. Single sign-on via Google Workspace, Microsoft 365 or SAML. UK data residency on request.
Can I cancel any time?
Yes — Scholar Monthly cancels at the end of your current billing month, no questions asked. Scholar Yearly can be cancelled with a pro-rata refund within the first thirty days.
Is there a bursary for low-income families?
Yes. A portion of every yearly subscription funds the Lesson Teacher Bursary. Talented students from low-income Commonwealth-country backgrounds may apply for free Scholar access at bursary@lessonteacher.io.
